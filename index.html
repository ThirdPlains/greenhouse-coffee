Our Menu

Here at Greenhouse Coffee Bros, we like to keep our menu short and sweet
to make sure our creations are made to perfection!

Daily Roast

Daily coffee brewed fresh every 15 minutes to ensure freshness. Our roasts will range from light to dark roast so be sure to ask at the counter for the brew of the day. We also have iced coffee for a hot summer day.

Espresso

Fair-trade and locally roasted. We take pride in making our espresso and make sure we make it just how you like it. Choose from Lattes, Cappucinos, and machiattos with one of our 8 syrup flavors.

Pour Over/French Press

Hot water that is hand-poured over grinds for a fuller and richer flavor or choose to make it in a caffettiera just like the italians. Hot water poured onto coffee grounds stepped for 4 minutes and served to perfection.


Cold Brew

Stepped for 24 hours for a rich and complex flavor profile. Your taste buds will be thanking you for this amazing taste. Perfect on a hot summer day or when you need to pull that all-nighter.

Teas and Kombucha!

We carry a variety of assorted green, black and herbal teas. Just ask the barista what kind of teas we have for the day which can also be served iced. Also when looking for a healthy and refreshing alterntive, kombucha is fermented tea made with sugar bacteria and yeast. Its also not just for hippies.

Food

We carry a variety of diffrent foods ranging from freshly made muffins and croissaints to a nice piece of herbed cream cheese avocado baguettes. We might not be french but we definitely have that french bakery charm.
